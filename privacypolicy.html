1. Introduction

At Farmily, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our blockchain-based agricultural supply chain platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Account information (name, email address, password)
  • Profile information (company name, role in the supply chain)
  • Product information (crop types, quantities, certifications)
  • Transaction data recorded on the blockchain

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Facilitate supply chain transactions and traceability
  • Improve and develop new features
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

4. Blockchain Data

Information stored on the blockchain is public and immutable. We take steps to ensure that sensitive personal information is not directly stored on the blockchain. Only necessary transaction data is recorded to maintain supply chain transparency.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information but cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@farmily.com.
